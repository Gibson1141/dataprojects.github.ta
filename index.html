WeCare Employee Attrition

This was an analysis carried out to determine the relationship between various factors like monthly income, job satisfaction etc., and how they influenced the employee attrition rate within WeCare. We discovered that good work-life balance and an increased environmental satisfaction could lead to a reduction in the attrition rate. This analysis was performed using Power BI.

This study was carried out to analyse the customer base of GoBikes and to figure out who their potential bike buyers are based on factors such as age group, income, marital status, etc. I was able to determin that people within the age ranges of 35 up till 54 were good potential buyers, amongst other generated insights. This analysis was done using Microsoft Excel
